Atlas Error with TextBoxWatermarkExtender and AutoCompleteExtender

July 30, 2006

This isn't really off-topic from my last post but I'll leave it at that for now...

I upgraded my Atlas Control Toolkit to the latest version and all of a sudden, I can no longer link a TextBoxWatermarkExtender and AutoCompleteExtender to the same textbox. I get a message: "Assertion Failed: Duplicate use of id..." etc, etc, and so on and so forth.

The problem occurs only in the current (July, I think) CTP of the toolkit. They are aware of the problem so my solution will be to sit and wait.
